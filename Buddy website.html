CTU-Buddy Educational

Welcome to the CTU buddy educational system website

If you are looking like this and struggling to understand your timetables and your work on your own, you have come to ther right place, we are an online study and class attendance platform serving as an invaluable tool for modern learners, offering convenience, interactivity, and efficient management of academic activities. With our array of features, we not only enhances the learning experience but also foster better communication and collaboration between students and instructors.

On our website you can find:

Timetable: On this page you will find a timetable that will help you with planning as well as class attendance.

Discussion: On this page learners can ask questions that will bw posted, these questions can be answered by the other learners and also your peers.

Share Resources: This page was created to share documents from in class that you missed or don't have, visit the page to request a specific file that you need.

About us: You can find out all about the creators of the website on this page.

Contact us: Contact the crew for help with the website or to report bugs or glitches found, we may also be able to help you with missing files that no one can give you on the share resources page.
